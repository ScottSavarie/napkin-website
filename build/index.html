Layer Controls

Add Rectangles, Photos from your camera roll, text labels and icons from a library to your canvas.

  • Add various layers to canvas
  • Layers snap to grid
  • Control status bar appearance

Canvas

Layout your mock by dragging and resizing layers. Closing the app will save your design for later.

  • Resize anything by dragging
  • Double tap anything to edit
  • Two finger tap to duplicate

Editing Controls

Edit the properties for any layer that you double tap on. You can also change the layer order, lock the layer, or delete it.

  • Control layer properties
  • Locking and layering
  • Deleting layers
